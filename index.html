Spirit of the Place

Spirit of the Place is an experimental project about creating a network of "Spirits" - artificially-intelligent entities scattered around our cities, with each Spirit personifying the character and representing the community of a single area

Spirits are like ghosts inhabiting places - buildings, streets, trees - but you can talk to them, ask them for recent local news, share a story. A Spirit can be interacted with by anyone who is in the place where the Spirit lives.

We are starting with creating the first Spirit in Dalston, London. Local residents will be able to interact with the Spirit using augmented reality (AR) technology on their phones.
